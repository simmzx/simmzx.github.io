I am a project assistant professor at The Institute of Statistical Mathematics (ISM) in Tokyo, Japan. I am also a visiting researher at The University of Tokyo Market Design Center (UTMD) in Tokyo, Japan. I completed my PhD from Techinical University of Munich (TUM).

My research interests focus on the operations management, specifically in the methodologies for operations research and their practical applications in real-world contexts. I had experience in queueing theory and online matching problems. Currently, I am expanding my knowledge in optimization algorithm design, machine learning and economic theory.

Beyond my research, I manage my own media channels. I am enthusiastic about documenting people's lives, introducing research topics, and sharing insights into diverse cultures.
